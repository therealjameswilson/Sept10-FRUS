# Avalon 9/11 Commission Report Source Chronology

Prepared May 21, 2026.

Source searched: Yale Law School Avalon Project PDF copy of *The 9/11 Commission Report*, 585 pages, at <https://avalon.law.yale.edu/sept11/911report.pdf>.

## Scope And Method

This sweep searched the full report text, with close review of chapter 6.5, "The New Administration's Approach"; chapter 7, "The Attack Looms"; chapter 8, "The System Was Blinking Red"; and the notes to chapters 6-8. The goal was not to restate the Commission narrative, but to extract a FRUS-style control chronology of events, meetings, and underlying document leads for a prospective counterterrorism volume covering January 20 to September 10, 2001.

The report is strongest as a source map when it names a contemporaneous document, meeting record, cable, intelligence article, memorandum, or briefing package. Later Commission interviews and meeting recollections are useful for annotation, but they should not substitute for the 2001 record where the underlying document can be located.

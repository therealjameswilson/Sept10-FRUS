# Google Drive memoir sweep

Date searched: May 21, 2026

Scope: user's Google Drive copies of memoirs by George W. Bush, Dick Cheney, and Condoleezza Rice. The public site does not expose private Drive URLs or copies of copyrighted PDFs. Only short quotation snippets and paraphrased source notes were added to the chronology.

## Drive files consulted

- George W. Bush, *Decision Points*; Drive result title: `Copy of Bush_Decision Points.pdf`.
- Dick Cheney with Liz Cheney, *In My Time*; Drive result title: `Cheney_In My Time.pdf`.
- Condoleezza Rice, *No Higher Honor*; Drive split-PDF results including `Rice0-70.pdf`, `Rice71-148.pdf`, and `Rice367-484.pdf`.

## Chronology additions

- `memoir-cheney-early-vp-pdb-process-2001-01-20`: Cheney's recollection of the January 20 inauguration, early vice-presidential role, integrated national-security staffing, and PDB access.
- `memoir-rice-clarke-strategy-green-light-2001-01-31`: Rice's recollection of Clarke's January 25 memorandum and her January 31 instruction to develop an al Qaeda strategy.
- `memoir-rice-july-domestic-warning-2001-07-05`: Rice's recollection of the late-May to mid-July threat spike, Tenet's July alarm, and the July 5 request for Clarke to convene domestic agencies.
- `memoir-bush-august-6-pdb-recollection-2001-08-06`: Bush's recollection of why the August 6 PDB was requested and how he understood its warning.
- `memoir-rice-august-6-pdb-recollection-2001-08-06`: Rice's recollection of the August 6 PDB and the homeland-threat framing during the Commission controversy.
- `memoir-rice-september-4-principals-recollection-2001-09-04`: Rice's recollection of the September 4 Principals review of covert action and Predator reconnaissance.
- `memoir-bush-massoud-assassination-recollection-2001-09-09`: Bush's recollection of Massoud's assassination and its effect on later Northern Alliance cooperation.
- `memoir-rice-september-10-strategy-forwarded-2001-09-10`: Rice's recollection that the strategy package was sent to Bush on September 10.

## Not added

- Bush's September 5, 2001 state dinner with Vicente Fox and later border-security reflection was not added as a counterterrorism chronology record because it is primarily immigration-policy context.
- Bush's recollection that his parents spent September 10 at the White House was not added because it does not materially advance the counterterrorism source map.
- Rice and Cheney post-September 11 recollections were not added except where they explain pre-September 11 records; the volume endpoint remains September 10, 2001.

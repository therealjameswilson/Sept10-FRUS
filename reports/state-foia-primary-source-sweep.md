# State FOIA Primary Source Sweep

Date searched: 2026-05-21; targeted gap follow-up 2026-05-23

Repository searched: Department of State FOIA Virtual Reading Room, Search Released Documents: <https://foia.state.gov/FOIALIBRARY/SearchResults.aspx>

The State search page uses the `Search2/SubmitSimpleQuery` API behind the browser UI. The sweep used a document-date window of January 20-September 10, 2001 and focused on Bin Ladin, al-Qaida, Taliban, Afghanistan, and worldwide caution terms.

## Useful Searches

- `Bin Ladin`, January 20-September 10, 2001: 54 hits. Useful case clusters included F-2010-03412, Bureau of Intelligence records related to Pakistani intelligence, the Taliban, al-Qaida, Afghanistan, or Osama bin Laden, and F-2011-03409, records related to Usama Bin Ladin.
- `"terrorist threat"`, January 20-September 10, 2001: 3 hits, including the April 20, 2001 S/CT briefing memorandum for the Deputies Committee meeting on terrorism and al Qaida.
- `"Worldwide Caution"`, January 20-September 10, 2001: 2 hits, the May 30 and June 22 State ALDAC public-warning cables.
- `"Arabian Peninsula"` and `"terrorist attacks in the Arabian Peninsula"`, July 2001: no exact State FOIA hit for the July 18 Arabian Peninsula warning located in this pass.
- Targeted May 23 API search for `C18605290`: 1 hit, `A/S Rocca's Meeting with Pakistan Ambassador Lodhi`, June 30, 2001.
- Targeted May 23 searches for State 111711, `Demarche on Threat by Afghan-based Terrorists`, and the exact July 18 Arabian Peninsula warning still did not locate direct State FOIA PDFs.
